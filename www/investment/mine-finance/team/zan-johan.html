Zan Johan

Managing Director

Mr. Johan is an experienced investment banker with particular focus on the metals and mining sector.

Mr. Johan worked fifteen years in investment banking, the last nine of which were at Deutsche Bank. Initially working as an M&A / corporate finance practitioner, Mr. Johan focused on advising clients in the natural resources sector. His investment banking transaction experience includes LBOs, cross-border M&A, debt restructurings, IPOs and project finance, with deal sizes ranging from US$40bn to US$50m. At Deutsche Bank, Mr. Johan was the global head of metals structuring where he was responsible for structured finance transactions in the metals and mining complex.

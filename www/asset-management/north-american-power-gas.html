The exploration of unconventional oil and natural gas fields in North America have had sweeping impacts on supply import/export dynamics, locational price differentials, and the shape of the financial markets for the natural gas and secondarily power markets. The downsizing of Investment Banking participation in trading, market making and credit intermediation has significantly decreased liquidity in the financial markets. Managing assets has, arguably, has never been more challenging.

TrailStone can help.

TrailStone North American Power

TrailStone Power NA prides itself on delivering a wide variety of services to the power industry for long-term and sustainable value creation.

TSH’s team of experienced professionals provide unique fuel procurement and incentive based asset management services as well as forward risk analysis and hedging solutions.
